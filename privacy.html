Privacy Policy for SBM Cheque Data

Effective Date: April 7, 2025

1. Introduction

SBM Cheque Data respects your privacy. This app is used to collect and manage cheque-related information securely and only by authorized users.

2. Data We Collect

  • User Email (via Google Sign-In)
  • Customer Name
  • Account Holder Name
  • Cheque Date
  • Cheque Amount
  • Submission Timestamp

3. Purpose of Data

The data is used for cheque collection tracking, reporting, and reconciliation in our internal operations only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third party. It is used internally and stored in secure Google Sheets.

5. Security

All data transmission is secured using HTTPS. Data is stored using Google Sheets under strict access control.
